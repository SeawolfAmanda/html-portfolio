Best Movies for a Lazy Weekend

Amanda's Top Picks for the best "do nothing" weekend

Stardust

Being a rom-com fantasy mix, this tale of a magic world beyond the wall is sure to catch your attention.

Showcasing multiple "box-office" names, including Robert DeNiro, this adventure of romance, murder, and magic is sure to catch the attention of most.

Practical Magic

It should be no secret Practical Magic is on this list, and coming in 2nd place at that.

Sandra Bullock and Nicole Kidman deliver an outstanding performance on love, relationships, sisterhood, and a bit of Witchcraft.

There's no way snuggling up with a fluffy blanket, hot cocoa, and Ms. Bullock and Ms. Kidman wouldn't bring peace and tranquility.

Hidden Figures

If you are unfamiliar with this knockout film, based on true stories regarding NASA and a group of black women, I recommend you don't even wait for a "do nothing" weekend to get this one in the books.

That's right, this movie is perfect for any occasion. Especially if you want to feel empowered, humbled, or enlightened, it covers all bases.

Based on a true story from our very own NASA during the cold war, times of segregation and sexism, a group of African-American Women mathmeticians took a large part in the successes of NASA.
